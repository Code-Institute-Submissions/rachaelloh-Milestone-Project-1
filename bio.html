About Jonathan

Jonathan graduated from NTU’s School of Art, Design, and Media in 2017, and has been freelancing as a graphic designer for seven years. He has worked on book layouts, painting commissions, album art for two local musicians, and finished his debut graphic novel, Walking Like Trees, a collaboration with singer-songwriter Inch Chua.
